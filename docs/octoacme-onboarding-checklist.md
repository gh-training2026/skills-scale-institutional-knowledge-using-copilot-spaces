# OctoAcme Role Onboarding Checklist

This checklist helps new team members get up to speed quickly based on their role. Complete all items in **General Onboarding** first, then follow the role-specific section for your position.

---

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-project-management-overview.md`
- [ ] Review `docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md` and identify your role and key interactions
- [ ] Gain access to project tooling (issue tracker, source repository, communication channels)
- [ ] Attend a team introduction meeting or schedule 1:1s with immediate collaborators
- [ ] Review the current project charter or one-pager
- [ ] Review the active sprint/iteration backlog
- [ ] Review the Risk Register for current known risks
- [ ] Confirm your role is listed in the project's RACI or team roster

---

## Developer

- [ ] Set up local development environment (see project README)
- [ ] Review coding standards and contribution guidelines
- [ ] Complete first PR with a small fix or documentation update
- [ ] Meet with Technical Lead for architectural overview
- [ ] Review existing test suite and coverage reports
- [ ] Understand the definition of done (DoD) for the team

---

## Technical Lead

- [ ] Review all current architecture decision records (ADRs)
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand roadmap priorities
- [ ] Meet with QA Lead to discuss test strategy and known risks
- [ ] Review open technical debt items in the backlog
- [ ] Identify existing standards and tooling gaps
- [ ] Schedule a team design review within the first sprint

---

## Product Manager

- [ ] Review the product roadmap and current OKRs or goals
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager to understand delivery timelines and dependencies
- [ ] Review open stakeholder feedback and unresolved requirements
- [ ] Meet with UX Designer to review active design work
- [ ] Confirm backlog prioritization approach with the team

---

## Project Manager

- [ ] Review all active project plans and milestone tracking
- [ ] Review the Risk Register and current escalation log
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager and Technical Lead for status alignment
- [ ] Confirm stakeholder communication schedule
- [ ] Review existing meeting cadence and recurring rituals
- [ ] Verify all roles are filled and hand-off processes are understood

---

## UX Designer

- [ ] Review existing design system and style guides
- [ ] Access and review current design files (e.g., Figma)
- [ ] Meet with Product Manager to understand user research backlog
- [ ] Review any existing usability testing reports
- [ ] Meet with Developers to understand implementation constraints
- [ ] Confirm design review cadence with the team

---

## QA Lead

- [ ] Review the current test plan and test coverage reports
- [ ] Access test management tooling and defect tracking systems
- [ ] Meet with Technical Lead to understand architectural testing needs
- [ ] Meet with Developers to review current CI/CD and automated test setup
- [ ] Review the definition of done and acceptance criteria standards
- [ ] Confirm the release readiness and go/no-go process with Project Manager

---

## Stakeholder (Business/External Partner)

- [ ] Receive project charter or one-pager from Project Manager
- [ ] Confirm communication preferences and availability for milestone reviews
- [ ] Review current requirements documentation and provide clarifications if needed
- [ ] Confirm sign-off process for releases and milestone deliverables
- [ ] Identify primary points of contact (Project Manager and Product Manager)

---

## Onboarding Follow-Up

After the first two weeks, confirm the following with your manager or Project Manager:

- [ ] You can access all required systems and tools
- [ ] You understand your responsibilities and key interactions (see `octoacme-roles-and-personas.md`)
- [ ] You have reviewed the hand-off process (`octoacme-handoff-template.md`)
- [ ] Any blockers to getting started have been addressed
